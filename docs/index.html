Digital System Design

I have a wealth of experience with Digital System Design using Hardware Description Languages. I am proficient in both Verilog and VHDL and I am comfortable using either Quartus or Vivado.

Analogue Circuit Design

Analogue Circuit Design is one of my favorite parts of my work. I offered Analogue Integrated Circuits and Instrumentation at university and have worked on numerous projects requiring analogue circuitry such as;

1. Designing a Network Analyser for measuring the gain and input impedance of amplifiers.

2. Designing key sensors to detect infrared pulses and decode radio signals for

3. Developing circuits to amplify and/or scale signals as needed.

Control Engineering

I took Control Engineering in my second and third years of university and have designed temperature control systems for laser drivers.

Software Development

Programming was a big part of my degree program and such, I have undertaken various software development projects utilising C++, Python, Martlab and F#

C++ projects include; Sudoku Solvers, Mastermind Codemaker and Solver, 2048 game

Python projects include; Sudoku Solver

Matlab projects include; Numerical methods for muultivariable integration

F# projects include; Visual design for digital circuit design software.

Microcontroller Programming

I have taken on many projects involving microcontrollers, programming them to perform tasks such as data acquisition and PID Control. One of my projects involved using a microcontroller to control the start-up and shutdown sequences of an instrument.
